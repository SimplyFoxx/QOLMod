QOL Bag Addon for WoW WotLK 3.3.5a (MAFWoW) by foks

Features:
Intelligent Junk Seller: Adds a "Sell Junk" button to every merchant window. You can set the threshold (from Greys all the way up to Epics) in the config. It automatically protects your Materials (Cloth, Ore, Herbs), Potions, and Recipes so you never accidentally sell your valuables.

Mathematical Bag Sorting: Uses a linear mapping system to reorganize your entire inventory with one command. Unlike standard sorters, it forces a specific, logical layout:

Gear & Weapons (Sorted by rarity and material)

Trade Goods & Reagents (Cloth, Leather, etc.)

Consumables (Food and Potions)

Whitelist Items (Your Hearthstone and essentials are locked to the very end of your bags).

Trash Unsellables: Includes an optional toggle to automatically destroy items with zero sell value or annoying leftover Quest items that vendors won't take.

Private Server Stable: Every action (selling/moving) has a built-in 0.1s delay. This prevents the "Internal Bag Error" and ensures you never get disconnected by the server's anti-spam protection.

Clean & Movable UI: A classic-style config menu (/qol) that you can move anywhere on your screen. No complicated setup—just toggle and go.

Macro Friendly: Easily trigger your bag sort with a simple /qol sort macro.

Commands:
/qol - Config menu
/qol sort - Sort Bags
